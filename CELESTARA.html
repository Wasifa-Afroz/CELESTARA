Your Journey Timeline

Pre-Launch (Week 1)

Complete medical screening, zero-gravity training, and Luminae cultural orientation at our Earth facility. Receive your personalized space travel kit and meet your crew.

Launch Day (Day 0)

Board the Celestara Voyager from our orbital station. Enjoy panoramic views of Earth before entering hyperdrive for the 72-hour journey through deep space.

Transit (Days 1-3)

Experience zero-gravity recreation, gourmet space cuisine, and educational programs about Luminara. Our crew ensures your comfort throughout the journey.

Arrival (Day 4)

Witness Luminara's triple moons as you descend through its bioluminescent atmosphere. Land at Starfall Port and meet your Luminae guide for orientation.

Exploration (Days 5+)

Begin your chosen adventure package. Each day brings new wonders from glowing forests to crystal caverns, with activities tailored to your interests and fitness level.

Return Journey

Depart with memories, photos, and your Crystalium keepsake. Arrive back at Earth's orbital station where transport to any global location awaits.
